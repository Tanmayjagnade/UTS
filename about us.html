Who We Are

Umakant Techno Services is a leading firm specializing in mechanical safety audits, risk assessment, and legal compliance. Our team of certified professionals ensures industries meet safety standards and regulations.

Periodical Testing of LPG /Co2 Bullets as per PESO Rules Storage Tank Calibration/ certification/approvals, Gas Cylinder Storage, RLA Inspection of IBR Boilers, Third Party Inspection Services, Construction Diagnosis (Using NDT technique as part of structural audits for certification of building Stability / Developing & Conducting Health Hygine Surveys and detail Studies for risk assessment (HIRA), preparation onsite emergency plan for company (including MAH) & Evaluations of safety / health for Industrial Safety etc as Govt. approved (DISH) Safety Auditor for Govt. of Maharashtra.
